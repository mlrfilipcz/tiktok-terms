About the App

This app helps you track the view count of your TikTok videos using Google Sheets automation. Each region in the Czech Republic is represented by a playlist, and views are automatically calculated for each region, reset every month, and recorded for historical tracking. Goals are set for each region, and the app provides insights on whether these goals are met.
